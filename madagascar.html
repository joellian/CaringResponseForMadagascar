OUR MISSION


Caring Response Madagascar Foundation (CRMF) supports programs that enable people to change their own lives by supporting programs that provide educational opportunities, lead to healthier communities, and foster economic self-sufficiency.

WHO WE ARE

Caring Response Madagascar Foundation (CRMF) is a U.S. based non-profit that supports programs that enable the poor to change their own lives. Our core projects emphasize education and literacy. Through these core programs and in connection with them, CRMF strives to impact not only the educational environment, but also the crucial areas of sanitation and hygiene, healthcare, and economic self-development.
